Development of a marketing website for a new song of Armaan Malik, this website has a tool to create an instagram story with a photo of the user a frame and part of the new song. the front-end was created with vanilla JS and Jquery with the backend created in Node JS - express and a tool for audio procesing and video creation.
Here is a video of the app working, you only need to update a photo and the website will create a short video with the song on the background and a frame in the picture about it.

You can see the impact of this website in tw under the hashtag #WishYouWereNext2Me in twitter
